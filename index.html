Strategic Core

The Concept: V-EEN-U

The name itself was the strategy. "V-EEN-U" honors the founder's mother, but phonetically structures the brand promise (We + Connection + You).

Live Concept
  • Two inverted mirrored 'e' shapes (Connection)
  • Continuous outline = Infinity (∞ - Trust)
